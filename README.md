## About

This repository contains study notes on phytomathematics and plant ecology. It documents an independent exploration of plant form, growth, and organization through geometry, topology, field observation, and programming.

The notes are neither academic research nor a summary of the scientific literature. The individual epistemic interest guiding the preparation and writing of these assorted hobby notes may be characterized as follows:

> *to cultivate a variational view of the spatial organization of plants (and of the leaf in particular) in interaction with processes of different geneses (for example, micrometeorological, physiological, and ecosystemic processes); in other words, a view of the leaf as a dynamic interface, or as a composition of organized processes with different geneses and constraints.*

The interest lies in developing such a view and, insofar as possible, documenting its development, making it accessible and open to others, as well as open to continuous transformation. (For a brief clarification of the “variational view,” see [leaf01](notes/leaf01-extraterrestrial-radiation.md).)
