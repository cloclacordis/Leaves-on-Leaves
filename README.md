## About

This repository contains study notes on phytomathematics and plant ecology. It documents an independent exploration of plant form, growth, and organization through geometry, topology, field observation, and programming.

The notes are neither academic research nor a summary of the scientific literature. They reflect a personal interest in understanding living plants — especially the leaf — as compositions of forms and processes.
